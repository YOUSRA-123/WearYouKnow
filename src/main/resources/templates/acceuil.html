Early stage of AD

In the mild dementia stage, people may experience: Memory loss of recent events. Individuals may have an especially hard time remembering newly learned information.

02

Moderate stage

During the moderate dementia stage of Alzheimer's disease, people grow more confused and forgetful and begin to need more help with daily activities and self-care.

03

Severe stage

In the final stage of this disease, individuals lose the ability to respond to their environment, to carry on a conversation and, eventually, to control movement.
